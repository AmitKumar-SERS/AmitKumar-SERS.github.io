Conference Presentations

Machine Learning-Enhanced airPLS Parameter Optimization for Noise-Robust SERS Baseline Correction

Type: Oral Presentation
Conference: ACS Spring 2026
Location: San Diego, CA, USA

Type: Poster Presentation
Conference: ACS Spring 2026 Sci-Mix
Location: San Diego, CA, USA

Type: Poster Presentation
Conference: ACS Spring 2026 Sci-Mix
Location: San Diego, CA, USA
Date: March 2026
